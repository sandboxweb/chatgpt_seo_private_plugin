About

This extension reveals the search queries used by AI systems:

  • ChatGPT: Intercepts and displays actual search queries
  • Google AI Overview: Uses Gemini 2.0 Flash for grounding queries
  • Google AI Mode (udm=50): Uses Gemini 2.0 Pro for better accuracy

The extension automatically selects the appropriate model based on the Google search mode for optimal query matching.
